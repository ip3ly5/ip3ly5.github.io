About

Lewis Burtt-Smith moved to Copenhagen from Portsmouth, UK. He studied Film and Television at South Downs College, then graduated in Multimedia Design at KEA. Proceeding to put these skills towards a practical environment in various workplaces.

Passionate about digital innovation, the move to Denmark was a natural fit. Lewis currently works as a Bartender for Lusso. In his free time, Lewis likes to train Judo at a local club. He also runs a youtube channel where he is currently working on a web-series Thoughtanaut.
