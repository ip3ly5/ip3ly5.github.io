About

Lewis Burtt-Smith moved to Copenhagen from Portsmouth, UK. With a background in film, he then graduated in Multimedia Design at KEA. Proceeding to put these skills towards a practical environment in various media workplaces. He has recently graduated from Web Development at KEA and works as a web developer for Friday

Passionate about digital innovation, the move to Denmark was a natural fit. When he isn't tinkering with the latest in VR web tech, Lewis likes to cycle and play basketball. He also runs a youtube channel where he is currently working on a web-series Thoughtanaut.
